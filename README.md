## Side project

### [FlipperHelper](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/flipperhelper/id6759716745)
iOS app I built in my free time for tracking purchases at flea markets and car boot sales. My wife resells secondhand items on eBay and Vinted and needed a better way to track what she paid, where she listed, and actual profit after all expenses.

Built with Swift, SwiftUI, SQLite (GRDB), offline-first architecture. Optional Google Drive sync for photo backup and CSV export. Free on the App Store, no ads.
